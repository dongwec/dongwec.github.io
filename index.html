About Me

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the department of mathematics at Colorado State University. I am working on frame theory and optimal transport. I also do research in applied analysis in turbulence and climate. I earned my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Clemson University. My postdoc mentors are Dr. Clayton Shonkwiler and Dr. Emily King. My PhD mentor is Dr. Martin Schmoll.
